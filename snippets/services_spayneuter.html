Low-Cost Vouchers

In our view, no companion cat should remain unaltered, and the best bet to seeing that a reality is to remove the financial obstacle for the pet owner. To that end, Purrfect Cat Rescue has partnered with local veterinarians to offer the public low-cost spay and neuter vouchers.

There are two types of vouchers:
  1. Tame ($50): intended for a social, family pet
  2. Feral ($30): intended for non-social cats stray and neighborhood cats

Non-tame cats should be taken to the vet appointment in a humane trap (see below). Additionally, they will be ear-tipped during sedation so they can be easily identified in future as a sterile cat.

We sell the vouchers at our weekend adoption events for cash or check—no credit/debit cards. The voucher cost covers the spay/neuter. At the the time of the vet appointment there is no additional cost unless you opt for vaccinations or other medical care. (Our vet in Union City offers low-cost vaccinations on specific days of the week should you want to vaccinate the cats you are spaying.)

The following vets honor our vouchers:
  • Irvington Pet Hospital, Fremont
  • Ardenwood Pet Hospital, Fremont
  • Veterinary Medical Center, Union City

Vouchers can be purchased at our local adoption events; check the home page for locations and times.
